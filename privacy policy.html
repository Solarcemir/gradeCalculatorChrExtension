Grade Calculator Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 09, 2025

The Grade Calculator Chrome extension is designed to help students manage and calculate their course grades. We are committed to protecting your privacy, and this policy outlines how we handle your data.

Data Collection

The Grade Calculator extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter, such as course names, weights, and grades, is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API (chrome.storage.local). This data is not shared with us or any third parties.

Data Usage

The data you input is used solely to perform grade calculations within the extension and to persist your entries between sessions. We do not use this data for any other purpose, nor do we transfer it to third parties.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services that collect data. All functionality, including grade calculations and theme toggling, is performed locally within the extension.

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend keeping your browser up to date to ensure the best protection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
